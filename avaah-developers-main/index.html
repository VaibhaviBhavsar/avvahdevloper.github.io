Consultation and initial meeting

The company meets with the client to discuss their needs, budget, and timeline. They may also visit the site to get a better understanding of the project.

2

Concept design

Based on the client's requirements, the company creates a concept design that outlines the overall vision for the project. This may include sketches, 3D models, or computer-generated renderings.

3

Design development

Once the client approves the concept design, the company begins to develop detailed drawings and plans. This may involve collaborating with engineers, contractors, and other specialists to ensure that the design is feasible.

4

Permitting and approvalss

Before construction can begin, the company must obtain the necessary permits and approvals from local authorities. This may involve submitting plans and documents for review and responding to any questions.
